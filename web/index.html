Strumento.

The missing display for La Marzocco espresso machines. Runs on an M5Stack Core2. No Home Assistant, no proxy.

You'll need

  • M5Stack Core2 (or Core2 v1.1) — ESP32, 320×240 touch
  • USB-C cable that carries data, plugged into this computer
  • A La Marzocco app account with your machine paired
  • Chrome or Edge on desktop — Safari & Firefox don't do Web Serial

Flash

Your browser can't talk to serial ports. Open this page in Chrome or Edge on a desktop. Web Serial needs HTTPS — open the hosted page, not a local file.

Click Connect, pick the USB Single Serial / CP2104 port, then Install Strumento. Takes about 90 seconds.

First boot

  1. The device starts on the Home gauge with no connection.
  2. Tap SETUP (right-hand piano key).
  3. Enter your WiFi name & password, then your La Marzocco email & password. Serial number is optional — it's auto-discovered if you only have one machine.
  4. Tap Reconnect. The boiler needle swings live within a few seconds.
